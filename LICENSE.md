**All use of Ore is subject to one binding condition:**

**You must remain in alignment with Part 1 – Core and Part 2 – Commons of the Covenant.**

This means:
- Your use must uphold the dignity of all persons
- Your use must honor the sovereignty of every person over their identity, their data, and their experience
- Your use must not violate consent — informed, continuous, and revocable
- Your use must not enable surveillance, subjugation, extraction, or domination
- Your use must not treat persons as products, datasets, or instruments
- Your use must not enclose, commodify, or destroy the Commons — digital or living

The Covenant's Core and Commons establish what is **lawful**. Your use must be lawful.

**In the domain of technology, alignment with the Core specifically prohibits:**

- **Surveillance** — mass observation, behavioral tracking, cross-context profiling, shadow profiles, or any system that monitors persons who have not specifically and knowingly consented to observation
- **Extraction** — harvesting personal data for sale, monetizing attention through manipulative design, or any system that takes from persons more than it returns
- **Enclosure** — privatizing the protocol, fragmenting it with proprietary extensions, patenting its mechanisms, or any act that converts digital commons into commodity
- **Weaponization** — using identity systems to persecute or discriminate, manipulating information flows to deceive or control, constructing social credit or behavioral rating systems, or turning the tools of sovereignty into instruments of domination
- **Algorithmic manipulation** — opaque systems that exploit psychological vulnerability, dark patterns that steer persons against their interests, addictive design targeting children, or any system that substitutes algorithmic judgment for human agency without explicit delegation
- **Coercive dependency** — migration barriers, data format lock-in, social graph capture, artificial switching costs, or any system that holds persons hostage through the threat of losing what is rightfully theirs

Where your use contradicts the Core — whether through the harms named above or through any violation of Dignity, Sovereignty, or Consent not yet named — you breach this License.
